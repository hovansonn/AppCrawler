predicted the online share should reach 5 by 2022 in a sector equivalent in size to Russias oil and gas market although without the benefit of providing foreign currency earningsFMCG is one of the largest markets in Russia it is comparable to the oil and gas sector to the banking sector and so on And as you can see we are here at the very start of our development he saidIf the estimates are
