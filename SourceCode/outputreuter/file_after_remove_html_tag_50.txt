REUTERS/Dado Ruvic/Illustration(Reuters) - Facebook Inc said on Thursday it would now share the report on its privacy practices to a newly formed committee on its board following its settlement with the U.S. Federal Trade Commission over data privacy probe. The social media giant said an independent, third-party assessor will report on its practices to the committee as well as the FTC.
